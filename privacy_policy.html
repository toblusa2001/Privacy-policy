Privacy Policy

The mobile game "Chubby's Road of Trials" (hereinafter referred to as "the game") respects the privacy of its users.

We do not collect any personal data directly. The game does not require user registration, account creation, or analytics. All game data (such as save files) is stored locally on the user’s device. The game may request permission to access device storage for saving purposes.

However, the game uses a third-party advertising service — Yandex Ads — which may collect non-personal technical information such as device identifiers, IP addresses, operating system version, app usage behavior, and other data required to display ads.

We do not control or take responsibility for how third-party services process such data.

This game is rated 12+. We do not target children under the age of 13. Parents are advised to monitor their children’s use of the app.

In the future, the game may include features such as multiplayer, accounts, or analytics. If so, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.
